Utkarsh Bhosekar is a data analyst in the CEPH Lab. He received his Bachelor of Engineering in Information Technology
from the University of Mumbai, India, and a Master of Science in Data Science from Indiana University, USA. His work
focuses on developing a computational pipeline for short-term forecasting of mosquito relative abundance. His research
combines and ensembles statistical and machine learning approaches to support situational awareness for mosquito
control authorities.
