Jessica Guerrini is a graduate research assistant in the CEPH Lab. She received her Bachelor of Science in Epidemiology
from Indiana University. She is currently obtaining her Master of Science in Biostatistics from Indiana University. 
Her research focuses on designing and testing alternative trial designs for mosquito control interventions using 
mechanistic modeling.
