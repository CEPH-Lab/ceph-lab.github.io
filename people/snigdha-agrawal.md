Snigdha Agrawal is a graduate research assistant in the CEPH Lab. She received her Bachelor of Science in Community Health from Indiana University. She is currently obtaining a Master of Public Health in Epidemiology from Indiana University. Her research leverages statistical modeling and machine learning to characterize the seasonal and diel behavior of mosquito vector species in the United States.
